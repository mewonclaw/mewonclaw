Privacy Policy

Your data. Your rules.

Last updated: April 28, 2026

TL;DR

  • We collect only what we need to run the service.
  • We never sell your data. Ever.
  • You can export, edit, or delete everything anytime.
  • We use industry-standard encryption.

1. What we collect

Account info: email or wallet address (whichever you used to sign in).

Your builds: the AI configs, prompts, and outputs you create.

Usage data: anonymous metrics (page views, build counts) to improve the product.

Optional: profile info you choose to add (handle, bio, avatar).

2. What we never do

  • Sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
  • Train our models on your private builds without consent
  • Read your conversations
  • Track you across the web

3. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Export all your data as JSON
  • Edit or correct any info
  • Delete your account and all data within 30 days
  • Object to specific processing

Contact us anytime to exercise these rights.

4. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We do regular security audits. If we ever have a breach, we will notify you within 72 hours.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login. We do NOT use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Changes

If we update this policy, we email everyone with active accounts at least 14 days before changes take effect.

Questions?

Reach out via Telegram or GitHub. We answer.
