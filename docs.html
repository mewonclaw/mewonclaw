What is MEW ON CLAW?

MEW ON CLAW is a free no-code playground for building your own AI. Whether you want a chat assistant, an autonomous agent, a single-purpose tool, or a multi-step workflow, you describe what you want in plain English and we generate it for you.

No API keys to wrangle, no infra to maintain. Just type, test, and share.

FAQ

Is it really free forever?

Yes. No tier upgrade. No hidden costs. Funded by a small team that thinks AI tools should not be paywalled.

Do I own my AI?

Yes. Your prompts, configs, and outputs are yours. You can export, delete, or migrate anytime.

Can I host it on my own domain?

Yes via embed code. Or use the API to integrate into your own product.

What models power my AI?

Top-tier models — GPT-4, Claude, etc. We pick the best fit for your use case automatically.
